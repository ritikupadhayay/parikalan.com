Sessions on Public speaking




Public speaking (sometimes termed oratory or oration) is the process or act of performing a presentation (a speech) focused around an individual directly speaking to a live audience in a structured, deliberate manner in order to inform, influence, or entertain them. Public speaking is commonly understood as the formal, face-to-face talking of a single person to a group of listeners. The points that were discussed are -
• Oratory
• It is the way the person speaks. His command on the voice and the fluency into it.
• The use of gestures
• Uses of gestures form an important part of Public Speaking for connecting with the audience.
• Control of the voice (Modulation)
• Using modulation of the voice, we can show various emotions and feelings that we want to convey.
• Vocabulary, register, word choice
• Right words at right time can do wonders. It can incite the audience and portray a sophistication in the speech.
• Using humor
• Using humor, we engage the audience to remain interested in the ongoing speech. Uses of puns, sarcasm, hyperbole, oxymoron are examples of humorous content.

The Class consisted of approximately 35 students, and the event was held was 15th Oct, 2015. It was held in Room No.9.
