ABOUT



PARIKALAN is Computer Science Society of PGDAV College with motto of Personality development of students and Boost the knowledge of all technically inclined students. We as Parikalan provide a productive space to anyone who wishes to manifest his/her ideas through the mediums of “Computer Logic”

We work more as a cooperative where we all pool in and share our talents and knowledge together so that we can benefit from each other in field of computer science. From Workshops to competitions encompassing Web development, Quiz, coding and much more. Our “Student Chapter Board” is constantly updated with weekly Brain Buster Quizzes. We are not just a society; we are a family, we are not just group of random people; we are enthusiasts, hobbyists and dreamers. Our dreams have led us to all of our innovative ideas and a step in helping students, from holding workshops on Robotics, Python and Public speaking to bringing compiled books and eBooks to every computer science student. Our “Student Attendance App” is a success in removing traditional methods of keeping track on student attendance using physical records. A festival is a day or period of celebration, well we celebrate our Annual Technical Festival “Xenium”. Xenium is an ancient Greek word stands for a present given to guests. We invite students and colleges from all over India as our guests to participate in various technical competitions and reward them with Prizes.
