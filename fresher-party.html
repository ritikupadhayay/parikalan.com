Fresh’em




Parikalan welcomed its new and promising batch of students with full enthusiasm. The senior students and newcomers of the college mingled with each other at the fresher’s welcome party held on 16th September 2015 in the college Seminar hall. Principal, Dr. M.M. Goyal inaugurated the event with the lighting of the ceremonial lamp along with the Department head, Dr. Arpita Agarwal and members of the Computer Science department of PGDAV College.
It was a fun filled event at which the fresher’s got an opportunity not only to showcase their talents and but also to interact with the seniors. Soon after the inauguration, the program kick-started with a ramp walk and introduction in which 36 contestants vied for the Mrs. And Ms. Fresher 2015 title. The contest included several rounds involving singing, dancing, talent display, and quiz, which wove the magic of fun and joy in to the event.
Based on their performance in the initial rounds, 16 of the 36 contestants moved in to the next rounds. In the second “Questionnaire Round”, the contestants had to prove that they were persons with beauty and brains and had the presence of mind and confidence to not only to give the correct answers but also to communicate these properly and effectively.
6 out of the remaining 16 were selected for the last “Talent Show” round. In the last round students had to showcase their talent in front of the audience.
At the end of the very enjoyable competition, Ms. Shruti Bansal was crowned as “Miss Fresher 2015” and Mr. Pratyaksh Prajapati was crowed “Mr. Fresher 2015”.
While amid the tough and breadth taking competition, special performances were given by the seniors and fun informal competitions were held to lighten and refresh the juniors.
After the competitions, it was for the fresher’s and seniors to rock the fresher party with dancing and grooving. Finally the program ended, leaving behind sweet memories that will be cherished lifelong.
