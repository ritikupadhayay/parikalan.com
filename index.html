A computer scientist is a person who fixes things that are not broken. The Department of Computer Science harbours some of the brightest young minds of PGDAV College. It encompasses all the spheres of computer science and gives a platform to foster the brilliance inside these talented students. Here we endeavour to generate a utopian virtual world, so that we can together weave a modern world of technology. Each and every member strives to create magic with their skills and thus, have just one motto “Coding Logic Creating Magic”. We not only emphasise on being the nerds and geeks that we already are but, also focus on using our knowledge in other spheres of life too.
